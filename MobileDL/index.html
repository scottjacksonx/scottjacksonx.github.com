MobileDL by scottjacksonx

MobileDL is Instapaper for video.

MobileDL lets you tell your Mac at home to download things for you to watch or listen to later. Just leave MobileDL running in the menubar on your home computer and use Simplenote on your iPhone, iPod Touch, iPad or any internet-enabled computer to give your computer URLs of videos to download. When you get home, the videos will be on your computer ready for you to watch.

MobileDL requires Mac OS X 10.5 or higher. If you're on a Windows or Linux box, check out mobile-dl, a more command-line-y version of MobileDL.
